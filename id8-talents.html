About ID8 Talents

We help empower theÂ next generation of student leaders

ID8 Talents is a student leadership program that brings together the top student leaders from 6 universities with 2 students selected from each and empowers them through various activities. The program is 4-month long, beginning in the middle of January 2022. During the program, students are expected to build and lead their own teams. They will also have access to top entrepreneur resources and expand their networks for the future.
